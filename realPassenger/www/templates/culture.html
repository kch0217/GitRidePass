RideSharing Culture

What is RideSharing?
RideSharing is an idea of sharing rides with others. As many private cars have unoccupied seats, offering these seats to passengers can help ease the tension of road usage. The utilization of resources can promote green living and ease the tension of road usage.
Why is this app developed?
The idea of ride-sharing has been suggested for years, yet the lack of ride-sharing platform leads to difficulties in bringing this culture into practice. Concerning this issue, this application is developed to serve as a platform for ridesharing.
What is this app for?
This application is specially designed for the HKUST community to share their ride freely. It aims at help solving the problem of long queues at bus stops during peak hours and shortening people's journey time. With this application, drivers and passengers who are going to travel between HKUST and Choi Hung/ Hang Hau can be matched and share their rides.
How to use this app:
You can offer rides using the driver app or requests for rides using the passenger app. You can set your requirement according to your needs, and then wait for matches. After being matched, notification will be made. When you want to pick up passengers or verify the driver, you can done it by showing your smartphone screen with the matching icon for identification since matched driver and passengers will share the same matching icon.
